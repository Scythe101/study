# Simple Future

## Uses

Use like in English, “Will do”

## Conjugation

Basically just take the present conjugation of haber, get rid of “h” then add an accent on everything except the nosotros form

| é | emos |
| --- | --- |
| ás | x |
| á | án |

## Examples

Haremos → We will do

Iré → I will go

Leerá → You will read
